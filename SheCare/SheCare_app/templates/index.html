Why WhatsApp? Because It Works.

In Kenya, WhatsApp isn't just an app – it's how we communicate. With over 90% of smartphone users already on WhatsApp, SheCare meets women where they are.

No new downloads. No complicated signups. No learning curves. Just send a message and get the health support you deserve, instantly.

Your conversations are private, secure, and encrypted end-to-end. Because your health conversations should stay between you and SheCare.
